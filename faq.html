JK Gaming will run games on the Ethereum and Polygon networks with plans to extend gaming to the Bitcoin network.

Adding liquidity to a pool seeds that pool meaning that from the start, a prize fund exists for gamers. The precise rules and protections to the liquidity provider are defined by the pool/game creator but many pools will pay back LP's first meaning no risk to the LP but providing less attractive payouts to gamers joinning early. Other pools/games may return strictly on a percentage basis meaning that some small risk of loss to liquidity providers but a better initial payout to gamers which should accelerate game ticket sales quickly, leading to bigger returns for the LP over the course of the game.

It depends entirely on the game ad the payout rules definned by the game. Some games will carry no risk to liquidity providers but will provide less initial incentive to gamers that could cause slower initial ticket sales and so lower returns to the LP. Other pools may split payout differently to better incentivise players and provide higher returns to LP's but at some risk to the LP.

In the interests of simplicity and time, initial games will be simple betting games based around random number generators (RNGs). However, we will be building out a family of gaming templates while encouraging the community to do the same which will lead to a growing list of games to play such as sports and eSports.

JK Gaming will provide a dedicated gaming portal for players but we will be working with gaming partners to enable games makers to provide their own gaming experience through their own sites and apps, using JK gaming at the backend. This gives pools and LP's the widest selection of games to back.
